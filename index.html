About Me

Hello! My name is Ubeid, though you might also know me as Ultikynnys or R60D. I’m passionate about creating games, tools, models, and tutorials, with a particular focus on 3D assets and character design. Thanks to my background in computer science, I’m equally comfortable with the programming side of game development.

Most of my projects have been developed using UE5, where I have the most experience, but I’ve also worked with Unity and, more recently, Godot, as game jams often demand flexibility with different engines.

From my early days of modding TF2 Source to collaborating with various teams on diverse games, I’ve gained a wealth of experience and valuable lessons that have shaped my broad understanding of game development.

If you’re interested in my work or have any questions, feel free to contact me.
